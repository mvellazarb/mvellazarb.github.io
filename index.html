I love composing music and I think that being creative on a regular basis is probably key to my long-term happiness. If I spend a significant amount of time away from composing I start to feel an insidious stress building up inside of me.

Currently I'm focusing on finishing my B.Sc. Computing Science degree at the University of Malta. I use Beeminder to ensure that I put in the hours. I've committed to doing 30 hours a week on university (that includes lectures).

I balance this time with practising drums, composing, gigging, assisting with music teaching at Opening Doors, rehearsing with various bands, exercising, reading, ear training, listening to music, journalling, art modelling.
